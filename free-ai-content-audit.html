The Problem

You have no idea whether AI search
knows you exist.

Traditional analytics don't track AI visibility. Here's what that blind spot costs you.

01

You're invisible to AI search and don't know it

When a potential client asks ChatGPT or Perplexity to recommend a provider in your industry, your name doesn't appear. You have no way to check this yourself at scale. Your Google Analytics won't show you what you're missing in AI-generated answers.

02

Your competitors may already be getting cited

AI models cite businesses with structured content, clear answer capsules, and strong topical authority. If your competitors have this and you don't, they're capturing demand you can't see in any dashboard. The gap widens every month you wait.

03

Compliance rules make content harder, not optional

In regulated industries, you can't just publish whatever ranks. AHPRA, TGA, ASIC, and APRA all have advertising restrictions. So you publish less, rank for less, and give AI models less to work with. Your compliance caution becomes an SEO disadvantage.

04

You're making strategy decisions without data

Should you invest in blog content? Restructure your service pages? Add schema markup? Without an audit, you're guessing. You might spend months optimising the wrong pages while the real opportunity sits untouched. You need a baseline before you can build a plan.
