Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026

This privacy policy explains how Amisha Sharma ("I", "me") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit commasandchaos.com ("the Site").

Information I Collect

Contact form submissions. When you fill out the contact form, I collect your name, email address, company name (optional), service interest, budget range (optional), and project details. This data is processed by Formspree and forwarded to my email.

Newsletter signups. If you subscribe to the blog newsletter, I collect your email address via Formspree.

Analytics data. I use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the Site. GA4 collects anonymized data including pages visited, time on site, device type, browser, and approximate location. This data is processed with consent mode enabled, meaning full tracking only occurs after you accept cookies.

Cookies. The Site uses cookies managed by CookieHub. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via the cookie banner.

How I Use Your Information

  • To respond to your project enquiries
  • To send you the newsletter you subscribed to
  • To understand how the Site is used and improve it
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing

I do not sell your personal data. Your information may be processed by these third-party services:

  • Formspree (form submissions) - Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics (website analytics) - Privacy Policy
  • CookieHub (cookie consent) - Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in my email for as long as needed to respond to your enquiry and for legitimate business purposes. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' default settings (14 months). You may request deletion of your personal data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data I hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for cookies at any time
  • Object to processing of your data

Security

The Site is served over HTTPS. I take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, email me at profamisha@gmail.com.
