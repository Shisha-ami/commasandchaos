My story

Here's something I've noticed about most content: it exists to fill a calendar. Not to do a job.

Written for algorithms. Approved by committees. Posted because it was Tuesday.

I write the other kind. The kind where every sentence earns the next one, every page has a single job to do, and the brand voice sounds like an actual person had an actual point of view.

"What I care about: E-E-A-T content, SEO that doesn't read like SEO, and making brands sound like themselves. Not like everyone else."

At CJ&CO, I build content systems every day. Website copy, landing pages, email sequences, Google ads, social ad copy, content strategy. The kind brands can actually execute. Because building a beautiful system nobody uses? That's just expensive decoration.

I'm also Editor-in-Chief of Audaz Revista, a publication I built from scratch covering Hispanic culture, business, and lifestyle. Before I launched it, I spent four years studying at Instituto Cervantes. Not because I had to. Because you can't write credibly about something you don't actually understand. That standard extends to every client I take on.

I hold a Digital Marketing certification from ISB, Indian School of Business. One of Asia's top-ranked institutions. I'm also Generative AI certified through Airtribe. I use AI to build smarter systems. The craft stays human.

Based in Delhi. Working with clients across Asia, Europe, and the Americas. Timezone-flexible. Async-friendly.

What I believe

Five things I'll never compromise on.

01

Every sentence earns the next. If it doesn't earn its place, it doesn't belong. This applies to landing pages. And to every word in your Google ad.

02

Brand voice is a product decision. Not a style guide footnote. When your content sounds consistent, customers trust you more. Full stop.

03

SEO and good writing are not in conflict. They have never been in conflict. If your SEO content reads like SEO content, the brief was wrong.

04

Depth beats volume. One page that genuinely helps your audience converts better than ten that don't. Every single time.

05

The best copy answers the question before the reader finishes asking it. That's not a trick. That's the entire job.
