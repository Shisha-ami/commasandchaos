My story

Good content has a job. I write the kind that actually does it.

I write copy where every sentence earns the next one, every page has a single job to do, and the brand voice sounds like an actual person with an actual point of view.

"E-E-A-T content, SEO that reads like real writing, and brands that sound like themselves. That's what I care about."

At an award-winning Australian digital marketing agency, I build content systems every day. Website copy, landing pages, email sequences, Google ads, social ad copy, content strategy. The kind brands can actually execute.

I'm also Editor-in-Chief of Audaz Revista, a publication I built from scratch covering Hispanic culture, business, and lifestyle. Before I launched it, I spent four years studying at Instituto Cervantes. I wanted to understand the culture deeply enough to write about it with real authority. That standard extends to every client I take on.

I hold a Digital Marketing certification from ISB, Indian School of Business. One of Asia's top-ranked institutions. I'm also Generative AI certified through Airtribe. I use AI to build smarter systems. The craft stays human.

Based in Delhi. Working with clients across Asia, Europe, and the Americas. Timezone-flexible. Async-friendly.

What I believe

Five things I'll never compromise on.

01

Every sentence earns the next. If it doesn't earn its place, it doesn't belong. This applies to landing pages. And to every word in your Google ad.

02

Brand voice is a product decision. It belongs in the strategy room, right next to pricing and positioning. When your content sounds consistent, customers trust you more. Full stop.

03

SEO and good writing are not in conflict. They have never been in conflict. If your SEO content reads like SEO content, the brief was wrong.

04

Depth beats volume. One page that genuinely helps your audience converts better than ten that don't. Every single time.

05

The best copy answers the question before the reader finishes asking it. That's the entire job.
